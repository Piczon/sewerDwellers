Biography

We are Sewer Dwellers - originating from Cracow, Poland. The name of our band derives from English expression used to describe residents of sewers in Bucharest since 1989. We embody a fresh perspective on the modern blend of deathcore and death metal. We draw inspiration from a variety of bands across the world such as Thy Art Is Murder, Fit For An Autopsy, Behemoth or Decapitated. The idea behind the band has been born a couple years back in the minds of the guitarists. With time we grew in numbers and ultimately evolved into the form you can listen to now on the album "Fate of a Maggot".
